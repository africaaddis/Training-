System Overview

The Peace & Security Management System is a comprehensive platform designed for the Addis Ababa Peace & Security Bureau. It manages peace keepers, crime cases, attendance, certificates, identity cards, and communication across all administrative levels of the city.

Who is this training for? This tutorial covers all user types from Blok users (field level) to Super Admins (system administrators).

System Hierarchy

City (Addis Ababa)
Sub-City (10 Sub-Cities)
District (Multiple per Sub-City)
Ketena (Multiple per District)
Blok (Multiple per Ketena)

User Roles at a Glance

Blok User

Field-level user - Manages a single block within a Ketena

Ketena User

Zone-level user - Manages a single Ketena

District User

District-level manager - Approves and manages

Sub-City Staff

Sub-City operational staff - Data entry and registration

Sub-City Admin

Sub-City administrator - Approves certificates, manages users

City User

City-level administrator - Full city-wide oversight

System Admin

Technical administrator - User management, logs, bulk operations

Blok User Training

Role Summary: Blok users are field-level personnel who manage a specific block within a Ketena. They are the closest point of contact for peace keepers.

Login and First Steps

  • 1

    Access the Login Page

    Open your browser and navigate to the system URL provided by your administrator.

  • 2

    Enter Your Credentials

    Username: Your assigned username
    PIN/Password: Your 6-digit PIN

  • 3

    Complete CAPTCHA (if shown)

    Enter the security code exactly as displayed. It is case-sensitive.

  • 4

    Click Login

    You will be taken to your personalized dashboard.

Your Dashboard

As a Blok user, your dashboard shows:

ComponentDescription
Welcome SectionShows your name, role (Blok), and location (City, Sub-City, District, Ketena, Blok)
Statistics CardsTotal peace keepers, crime cases, active members, pending members in your Blok
Quick ActionsView Peace Keepers, View Crime Cases, QR Attendance, Request Identity Card

Registering a Peace Keeper

  • 1

    Navigate to Register Peace Keeper

    Click Register Peace Keeper in the sidebar menu.

  • 2

    Fill Registration Information

    Member Type: Select "Peace Keepers" (most common)
    Round: Select the appropriate round (1-8)

  • 3

    Fill Personal Information

    Full Name: Complete name of the peace keeper
    Gender: Male or Female
    Age: Must be 18 or older
    Date of Birth: Ethiopian calendar date (dd/mm/yyyy)
    Birth Place: Where they were born

  • 4

    Address Information (Auto-filled)

    Ketena: Auto-filled with your Ketena
    Blok: Auto-filled with your Blok
    House No: Enter the peace keeper's house number
    Ethnic Group: Select their ethnicity

  • 5

    Contact Information

    Telephone: Must be 09xxxxxxxx format
    Emergency Contact Name: Full name of emergency contact
    Emergency Telephone: Emergency phone number
    Emergency Address: Emergency contact address

  • 6

    Upload Photo

    Click the upload area, select a photo (JPG/PNG, max 5MB), wait for preview.

  • 7

    Submit

    Click Register Peace Keeper and wait for confirmation. The system generates a unique ID.

Pro Tip: If you enter Age first, the system will suggest a Date of Birth automatically!

Registering a Crime Case

  • 1

    Navigate to Register Crime

    Click Register Crime in the sidebar menu.

  • 2

    Case Details

    Case Name: Type the crime name (auto-suggest available)
    Description: Provide detailed incident description

  • 3

    Date and Time

    Date: Ethiopian calendar date (dd/mm/yyyy)
    Time: Time of incident

  • 4

    Location (Auto-filled)

    Ketena: Auto-filled with your Ketena
    Blok: Auto-filled with your Blok

  • 5

    Response and Status

    Initial Response: Action taken (e.g., Police Alerted)
    Current Status: Open/Under Investigation/Closed
    Investigation Start Date: Ethiopian date
    Informed By: Source of report

  • 6

    Submit

    Click Submit Crime Case to save.

QR Attendance Check-In/Out

  • 1

    Open Insert Attendance

    Click Insert Attendance in the sidebar.

  • 2

    For Check-In

    Select Check In tab → Click Start Scanner → Allow camera access → Scan QR code → Click Check In

  • 3

    For Check-Out

    Select Check Out tab → Enter peace keeper ID → Click Check Out

Important: Each peace keeper can only check in ONCE per day. The system prevents duplicate check-ins.

Sending SMS to Peace Keepers

  • 1

    Click Send SMS

    Navigate to Send SMS in the sidebar.

  • 2

    Compose Message

    Type your message (max 160 characters per SMS). Character count is shown.

  • 3

    Select Recipients

    Click Select Peace Keepers → Check boxes next to names → Click Add Selected

  • 4

    Send

    Click Send SMS to deliver the message.

Sub-City Admin Training

Role Summary: Sub-City Admins are senior administrators who oversee all districts in their sub-city. They approve certificate requests, manage district users, and have full visibility of their sub-city.

Certificate Management (Most Important)

As a Sub-City Admin, you are the FIRST approver for certificate requests from districts.

  • 1

    Open Manage Certificates

    Click Manage Certificates in the sidebar.

  • 2

    Review Pending Requests

    Click Requested tab to see certificate requests awaiting YOUR approval.

  • 3

    Preview Certificate

    Click Preview to see how the certificate will look.

  • 4

    Approve or Reject

    Click Approve to send to City for final approval, or Reject with reason.

Certificate Workflow: District requests → YOU approve → City approves → Certificate ready for printing.

User Management (District Users)

You manage District user accounts in your sub-city.

  • 1

    View District Users

  • 3

    Manage User

    Click Manage to edit user status, name, phone, etc.

  • Analyze Peace Keepers & Crimes: Generate sub-city wide reports

Common Features for All Users

These features are available to most user types (with varying permissions).

Searching and Filtering

Search Bar: Type any keyword (name, ID, phone number) to filter results instantly.

Advanced Filters: Use dropdown filters to narrow down by Sub-City, District, Ketena, Blok, date range, or status.

Clear Filters: Click the Reset button to clear all filters.

Exporting Data

CSV Export: Download data as CSV for analysis in Excel.
Excel Export: Download as Excel file for reports.
Print: Print the current view directly from your browser.

Changing Language

Click the language button in the top bar (shows current language) → Select your preferred language (English, Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, or Tigrinya). The interface switches immediately.

Changing Theme

Click the theme button (palette icon) in the top bar → Select Light, Dark, High Contrast, Sepia, or Blue Light theme.

Notifications

The bell icon 🔔 shows unread message count. Click to see recent notifications. Clicking a notification takes you to the related page.

Office Communication

Internal messaging system for communicating with other users in your scope. Click Office Communication → Compose → Select recipients → Write message → Send.

Updating Your Account

Click Update Account to change your full name, telephone, password, and profile picture. Click Update More Information for additional personal details.
